Radio Voice Coaching

Press “Analyze My Voice” after recording, or use the demo score now.

  • Slow down your first sentence by 10%.
  • Drop your pitch at the end of statements.
  • Pause after the words “I’ll be quick.”
  • Ask one clean question, then stop talking.

Daily Radio Voice Warmups

Five quick drills to help you sound clear, steady, and confident before outbound calls.

Smile TestSay the line twice: once flat, once smiling. Hear the warmth change.
Slow Down DrillRead the opener 10% slower so the first seven seconds feel calm.
Lower The EndingStop ending statements like questions. Sound certain, not unsure.
Pause DrillSay one sentence. Pause. Let the person process before you continue.
Warm AuthorityCombine friendly tone with a direct question and a confident stop.

Before / After Confidence

After

Clear, respectful, confident, and easy to answer.
